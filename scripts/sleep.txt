# HPYNO Sleep Session
#
# Goal: guide the user into sleep. No emergence — voice fades to nothing.
# The user should barely notice when they cross from listening to sleeping.
#
# Key principles:
# - No gates or decisions — nothing that requires conscious thought
# - Voice gets progressively quieter and slower
# - Longer pauses as we go deeper — let silence do the work
# - Body scan creates heaviness — weight is sleep's doorway
# - Final stage fades to pure ambient
# - No emergence — the session ends in silence

[INTERACTIVE: prompt sleep_ready]
find a comfortable position...
close your eyes... when you're ready.

[STAGE: settle]
good...
you've chosen to rest.
[SLICE]
the day is behind you now...
all of it... done.
[SLICE]
whatever happened today...
you can set it down.
[PAUSE 2]
[CMD it doesn't need you anymore]
[SLICE]
the only thing that matters...
is right here...
this bed... this warmth... this breath.
[SLICE]
you don't need to try to sleep...
sleep will come... on its own...
[CMD when you stop trying]
[INTERLUDE 5]

[STAGE: drift]
notice where your body meets the bed...
every point of contact...
[CMD sinking... just a little]
[SLICE]
your feet... let them be heavy...
heavier than you'd expect...
as if they're made of warm sand...
slowly settling.
[PAUSE 2]
[SLICE]
your legs... the weight of them...
you might notice... you've forgotten them already...
that's good... [CMD let them go]
[SLICE]
your hips... your lower back...
pressing into the mattress...
the bed is holding you...
[CMD you don't need to hold yourself]
[PAUSE 2]
[SLICE]
your stomach... softening...
each breath... a slow wave...
rising... falling... rising... falling...
without any effort.
[SLICE]
your chest... your shoulders...
dropping... melting...
[CMD heavier... and heavier]
[SLICE]
your arms... your hands...
you might notice a tingling...
or a warmth... or nothing at all...
all of that is fine.
[PAUSE 3]
[SLICE]
your neck... your jaw...
[CMD let it all go]
your face... smoothing...
forehead... softening...
every tiny muscle... releasing.
[INTERLUDE 8]

[STAGE: sinking]
you're sinking now...
and that's exactly right.
[SLICE]
notice how comfortable this is...
how easy it is... to just... be here.
[PAUSE 2]
[SLICE]
thoughts might drift through...
like clouds... passing...
you don't need to follow them...
[CMD just let them go]
[SLICE]
each breath takes you deeper...
not because you're trying...
[CMD because you've stopped trying]
[PAUSE 3]
[SLICE]
the sounds around you...
are becoming distant...
everything... moving further away...
[CMD except this voice... and soon... even this]
[PAUSE 3]
[SLICE]
you're somewhere between...
awake and asleep...
and that place... is perfect.
[CMD stay here... as long as you like]
[INTERLUDE 12]

[STAGE: dissolve]
so heavy now.
[PAUSE 3]
[SLICE]
so warm.
[PAUSE 3]
[SLICE]
[CMD drifting]
[PAUSE 4]
[SLICE]
nothing to do.
[PAUSE 3]
[SLICE]
nothing to think.
[PAUSE 3]
[SLICE]
[CMD just... letting go]
[PAUSE 5]
[SLICE]
the bed... holds you.
[PAUSE 4]
[SLICE]
[CMD sleep... is here]
[INTERLUDE 15]

[STAGE: sleep]
[CMD sleep]
[PAUSE 8]
[SLICE]
[CMD rest]
[PAUSE 8]
[SLICE]
[CMD safe]
[INTERLUDE 45]
